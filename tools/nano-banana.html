The "Nano Banana" Story

The model first appeared anonymously on LMArena, a crowdsourced AI evaluation platform, under the mysterious name "nano-banana." It quickly rose to become the world's top-rated image editing model.

On August 26, 2025, Google DeepMind revealed the mystery: Nano Banana was their latest Gemini 2.5 Flash Image model all along.

The "Nano Banana" nickname comes from a code example in Google's documentation: "Create a picture of my cat eating a nano-banana in a fancy restaurant under the gemini constellation."
