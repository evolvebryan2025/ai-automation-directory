What Google Says

"We built it for rapid visual exploration, not pixel-perfect edits."

Whisk is designed as a creative ideation tool. It captures the essence of your subjects rather than creating exact replicas, making it ideal for brainstorming and exploring visual concepts quickly.

Behind the scenes, Gemini automatically generates detailed captions from your input images, which Imagen 3 then uses to create new images. You can view and edit these prompts at any time for more control.
