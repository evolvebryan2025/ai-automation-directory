How Midjourney Works

1. Join Discord

Sign up and join the official Midjourney Discord server where all generation happens.

2. Write Your Prompt

Use the /imagine command followed by your text description of what you want to create.

3. AI Generates Images

Midjourney's AI processes your prompt and generates four unique image variations.

4. Upscale & Refine

Select your favorite variation and upscale it for higher resolution or create variations.
